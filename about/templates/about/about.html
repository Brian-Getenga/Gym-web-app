About Us

Jerima Club is dedicated to teaching the art of karate and promoting personal growth. We offer top-notch training and a community that supports both physical and mental development.

Our Mission

At Jerima Club, our mission is to empower individuals through martial arts, foster a strong community, and promote a healthier lifestyle. Our training programs are designed to build discipline, confidence, and physical fitness.

Our History

  • Founded in 2000

    Jerima Club was established to promote martial arts in the community.

  • First National Championship

    We won our first national championship in 2005, marking a major milestone.

  • Expanded Training Programs

    In 2010, we expanded our training programs to include various martial arts disciplines.
